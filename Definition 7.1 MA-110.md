# Definition of coset products

> [!definition] Definition 7.1 MA-110 - coset products
> Let $I$ be an ideal of a ring $R$, and let $a,b \in R$. The **coset product** of $a+I$ and $b+I$ is the coset $ab+ I$. That is, $(a+I)(b+I)=ab+I$.
