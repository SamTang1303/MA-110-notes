# Problem 41.3 MA-111

> [!problem] Problem 41.3
> Describe $C_{i}(P),Z_{i}(P),B_{i}(P)$, and $H_{i}(P)$ for the space consisting of just the 0-simplex $P$.

$$
\begin{align}
C_{i}(P) &= \{ nP \mid n \in  \mathbb{Z} \} \simeq \mathbb{Z} \\
Z_{i}(P) &= P \\
B_{i}(P) &= 0 \\
H_{i}(P) &= P.
\end{align}
$$
